Exercise 3.

Let \(A =\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 3 \\ \end{array} \right)\) . Find a matrix \(B\) such that \(A B = I\) and \(B A = I\text{,}\) where \(I = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \\ \end{array} \right)\text{.}\)
Answer.
\(\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1/2 & 0 \\ 0 & 1/3 \\ \end{array} \right)\)
