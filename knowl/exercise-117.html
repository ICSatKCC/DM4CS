\begin{equation*} S = \{HHH,\ HHT,\ HTH,\ HTT,\ THH,\ THT,\ TTH,\ TTT\} \end{equation*}
(see Example 3.1.5 and Example 3.1.6).

  2. \(E = \{HHH,TTT\}\text{.}\)
  3. \(E = \{HHT,HTH,THH\}\text{.}\)
  4. \(E = \{HHT,HTH,HTT,THH,THT,TTH,TTT\}\text{.}\)
What are the probabilities of each of these events?
