It can be shown that a lattice is nondistributive if and only if it contains a sublattice isomorphic to one of the lattices in Figure 12.2.6. The Hasse diagram on the right of this figure, produces the diamond lattice, which is precisely the one that is defined in Example 12.2.5. The lattice based on the left hand poset is called the pentagon lattice.
