Let \(a\) and \(b\) be positive real numbers, and \(r\) a real number.
\begin{gather} \log_2 1 = 0\tag{16.2.4}\\ \log_2 a b = \log_2a + \log_2b\tag{16.2.5}\\ \log_2 \frac{a}{b}= \log_2a - \log_2b\tag{16.2.6}\\ \log_2a^r = r \log_2a\tag{16.2.7}\\ 2^{\log_2a}= a\tag{16.2.8} \end{gather}
