Exercise 7.

Let \(A = \{+,-\}\) and \(B = \{00, 01, 10, 11\}\text{.}\)
  1. List the elements of \(A \times B\)
  2. How many elements do \(A ^4\) and \((A \times B)^3\) have?
Answer.
  1. \(\displaystyle \{+00, +01, +10, +11, -00, -01, -10, -11\}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle 16 \textrm{ and } 512\)
