Item 11.3.3.1.a.
\(f\) is injective:
\begin{equation*} \begin{split} f(x) = f(y) & \Rightarrow a \bullet x = a \bullet y \\ & \Rightarrow x = y\textrm{ by left cancellation}\\ \end{split} \end{equation*}
.
\(f\) is surjective: For all \(b \in G\text{,}\) \(f(x) = b\) has the solution \(a^{-1}\bullet b\text{.}\)
