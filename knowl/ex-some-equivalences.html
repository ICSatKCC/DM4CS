Example 4.3.7. Some Equivalences.

The following are all equivalences:
  1. \((p \land q)\lor (\neg p \land q)\iff q\text{.}\)
    \begin{equation*} \begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|} \hline p & q & (p\land q) & \neg p\land q &\neg (p\land q) \lor (\neg p\land q) & \neg (p\land q) \lor (\neg p\land q) \leftrightarrow q \\\hline 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\\hline 0 & 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\\hline 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\\hline 1 & 1 & 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\\hline \end{array} \end{equation*}
  2. \(p \to q \iff \neg q \rightarrow \neg p\)
    \begin{equation*} \begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|} \hline p & q & (p\rightarrow q) & \neg q\rightarrow \neg p & p \to q \leftrightarrow \neg q \rightarrow \neg p\\\hline 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\\hline 0 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\\hline 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\\hline 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\\hline \end{array} \end{equation*}
  3. \(p \lor q \iff q \lor p\text{.}\)
    \begin{equation*} \begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|} \hline p & q & p \lor q & q \lor p & (p \lor q)\leftrightarrow (q \lor p)\\\hline 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 &1 \\\hline 0 & 1 & 1 & 1 &1\\\hline 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 &1 \\\hline 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 &1\\\hline \end{array} \end{equation*}
