Item 10.3.4.12.c.
A compatible with respect to relation \(R\) is any subset \(Q\) of set \(S\) such that \(x \in Q \textrm{ and } y \in Q \Rightarrow (x, y) \in R\text{.}\) A compatible \(g\) is a maximal compatible if \(Q\) is not a proper subset of another compatible. Give all maximal compatibles with respect to relation \(R\) defined above.
