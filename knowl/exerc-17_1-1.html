Exercise 1.
Let \(S = \{\varep, ab, abab\}\) and \(T = \{aa, aba, abba, abbba, \ldots\}\text{.}\) Find the following:
  1. \(\displaystyle S^2\)
  2. \(\displaystyle S^3\)
  3. \(\displaystyle S^*\)
  4. \(\displaystyle ST\)
  5. \(\displaystyle TS\)
Answer.
  1. \begin{equation*} S^2 = \{\varep, ab, abab, ababab, abababab\} \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} S^3 = \{\varep, ab, abab, ababab, abababab, ababababab, abababababab, ababababababab, abababababababab \} \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} S^* = \{\varep, ab, abab, ababab, abababab, \ldots \} \end{equation*}
  4. \begin{equation*} ST = \{aa, aba, abba, abbba, \ldots, abaa, ababa, ababba, \ldots , ababaa, abababa, abababba, \ldots\} \end{equation*}
  5. \begin{equation*} TS = \{aa, aba, abba, abbba, \ldots, aaab, abaab, abbaab, \ldots , aaabab, abaabab, abbaabab, \ldots\} \end{equation*}
