Answer 5.2.4.3.1.

Proof:
  • Basis: \(1=1(2)(3)/6=1\)
  • Induction:
    \begin{equation*} \begin{split} \sum_1^{n+1} k^2 &=\sum_1^n k^2+(n+1)^2\\ &=\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}+(n+1)^2\\ &=\frac{(n+1)(2n^2+7n+6)}{6}\\ &=\frac{(n+1)(n+2)(2n+3)}{6}\textrm{ }\blacksquare\\ \end{split} \end{equation*}
