Exercise 3.

Let \(p(x) = x^5+ 3x^4 - 15x^3 + x - 10\text{.}\)
  1. Write \(p(x)\) in telescoping form.
  2. Use a calculator to compute \(p(3)\) using the original form of \(p(x)\text{.}\)
  3. Use a calculator to compute \(p(3)\) using the telescoping form of \(p(x)\text{.}\)
  4. Compare your speed in parts b and c.
Answer.
  1. \(p(x)\) in telescoping form: \(((((x+3)x-15)x+0)x+1)x-10\)
  2. \(\displaystyle p(3)=((((3+3)3-15)3-0)3+1)3-10=74\)
