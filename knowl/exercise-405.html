Exercise 5.

What is a reasonable definition of the term sublattice?
Answer.
One reasonable definition would by this: Let \([L; \lor, \land ]\) be a lattice and let \(K\) be a nonempty subset of \(L\text{.}\) Then \(K\) is a sublattice of \(L\) if and only if \(K\) is closed under both \(\lor\) and \(\land\)
