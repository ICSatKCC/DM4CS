Find the language accepted by the DFA shown below (and describe it using a regular expression!) The start state of \(M\) is accepting, which means \(\varep \in L(M)\text{.}\) If \(M\) is in state \(q_0\text{,}\) a sequence of two \(a\)’s or three \(b\)’s will move \(M\) back to \(q_0\) and hence be accepted. So \(L(M) = L((aa | bbb)^*)\text{.}\)
