Let \(S = \{01, ba\}\text{.}\) Then
\begin{align*} S^0 \amp = \{\varep\}\\ S^1 \amp \{01, ba\} \\ S^2 \amp \{0101, 01ba, ba01, baba\}\\ S^3 \amp = \{010101, 0101ba, 01ba01, 01baba, ba0101, ba01ba, baba01, bababa\} \text{ etc, so} \\ S^* \amp =\{\varep,01,ba,0101,01ba,ba01,baba,010101,0101ba,\ldots\}. \end{align*}
