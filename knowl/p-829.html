The event we are interested in is
\begin{equation*} A = \{ (i,j) \in S : i+j \text{ is even} \} . \end{equation*}
Observe that \(i+j\) is even if and only if both \(i\) and \(j\) are even or both \(i\) and \(j\) are odd. Therefore, we split the event \(A\) into two disjoint events
\begin{equation*} A_1 = \{ (i,j) \in S : i,j \text{ are both even} \} \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} A_2 = \{ (i,j) \in S : i,j \text{ are both odd} \} . \end{equation*}
