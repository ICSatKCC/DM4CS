Definition 4.1.6. Conditional Statement.

The conditional statement “If \(p\) then \(q\text{,}\)” denoted \(p \rightarrow q\text{,}\) is defined by the truth table:
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{|c|c|c|}\hline p & q & p \rightarrow q \\ \hline 0 & 0 & 1 \\\hline 0 & 1 & 1 \\\hline 1 & 0 & 0 \\\hline 1 & 1 & 1 \\\hline \end{array} \end{equation*}
