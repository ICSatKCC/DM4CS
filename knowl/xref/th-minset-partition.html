Theorem 6.3.8. Minset Partition Theorem.

Let \(A\) be a set and let \(B_1\text{,}\) \(B_2\) \(\ldots\) , \(B_n\) be subsets of \(A\text{.}\) The set of nonempty minsets generated by \(B_1\text{,}\) \(B_2\) \(\ldots\) , \(B_n\) is a partition of \(A\text{.}\)
