It should be fairly clear that every language that is accepted by a DFA is also accepted by an NFA. Pictorially, a DFA looks exactly like an NFA (an NFA that doesnâ€™t happen to have any \(\varep\)-transitions or multiple same-label transitions from any state), though there is slightly more going on behind the scenes.
