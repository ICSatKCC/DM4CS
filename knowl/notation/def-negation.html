Definition 4.1.5. Logical Negation (NOT).

If \(p\) is a proposition, its negation, \(\textrm{not } p\text{,}\) denoted \(\neg p\text{,}\) and is defined by the truth table
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{|c|c|} \hline p & \neg p \\ \hline 0 & 1 \\\hline 1 & 0 \\\hline \end{array} \end{equation*}
The statement \(\neg p\) is true if \(p\) is false.
