Item 6.2.4.3.d.
\begin{equation*} \begin{split} A\cap (B-C) &=A\cap (B\cap \overline{C}) \\ & = (A\cap B\cap \overline{A})\cup (A\cap B\cap \overline{C}) \\ & =(A\cap B)\cap (\overline{A}\cup \overline{C}) \\ & =(A\cap B)\cap \overline{(A\cup C)} \\ & =(A-B)\cap (A-C) \quad \square\\ \end{split} \end{equation*}
