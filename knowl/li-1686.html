Item 11.5.5.6.c.
Prove that if \(H \leq G\) and \(S \subseteq H\text{,}\) then \(\langle S \rangle\leq H\text{.}\) This proves that \(\langle S \rangle\) is contained in every subgroup of \(G\) that contains \(S\text{;}\) that is, \(\langle S \rangle =\underset{S\subseteq H, H\leq G}{\cap }H\) .
