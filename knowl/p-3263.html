An algebraic system is a mathematical system consisting of a set called the domain and one or more operations on the domain. If \(V\) is the domain and \(\bullet _1, \bullet _2, \ldots , \bullet _n\) are the operations, \(\left[V;\bullet _1, \bullet _2, \ldots , \bullet _n\right]\) denotes the mathematical system. If the context is clear, this notation is abbreviated to \(V\text{.}\)
