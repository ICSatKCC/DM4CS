A monoid is a set \(M\) together with a binary operation \(\bullet \) with the properties
  1. \(\bullet \) is associative: \(\forall a,b,c \in M\text{,}\) \((a\bullet b)\bullet c=a\bullet (b\bullet c)\) and
  2. \(\bullet \) has an identity in \(M\text{:}\) \(\exists e\in M\) such that \(\forall a \in M\text{,}\) \(a\bullet e=e\bullet a=a\)
