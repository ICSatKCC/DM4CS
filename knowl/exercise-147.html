Exercise 3.

Rewrite the following with as few extraneous parentheses as possible:
  1. \(\displaystyle (\neg ((p) \land (r))) \lor (s)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle ((p) \lor (q)) \land ((r) \lor (q))\)
Answer.
  1. \(\displaystyle \neg (p\land r) \lor s\)
  2. \(\displaystyle (p\lor q) \land (r\lor q)\)
