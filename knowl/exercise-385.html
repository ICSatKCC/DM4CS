Exercise 7.

You have a system of \(k\) equations in two variables, \(k \geq 2\text{.}\) Explain the geometric significance of
  1. No solution.
  2. A unique solution.
  3. An infinite number of solutions.
Solution.
  1. No solution. The lines are parallel.
  2. A unique solution. The lines must intersect.
  3. An infinite number of solutions. The lines must be the same line.
