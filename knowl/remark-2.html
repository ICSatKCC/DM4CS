Remark 17.4.6. A pause before we continue:.
Note that for the simplest regular expressions, the machines that we created to accept the languages generated by the regular expressions were in fact DFAs. In our last case above, however, we needed \(\varep\)-transitions to build the new machine, and so if we were trying to prove that every regular language could be accepted by a DFA, our proof would be in trouble.
