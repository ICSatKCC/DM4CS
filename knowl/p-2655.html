Here we take the absolute value of our polynomial \(\lvert 6x^4 - 2x^3 + 5 \rvert\) and multipling all of the constant cofficients by \(x^4\) we have:
\begin{equation*} \lvert 6x^4 - 2x^4 + 5x^4 \rvert = 6x^4 + \lvert 2x^4 \rvert + 5x^4 = 13x^4. \end{equation*}
Therefore, we have
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c c} 6x^4 - 2x^3 + 5 & \leq 13x^4 \\ & = O(x^4) \end{array} \end{equation*}
With \(c = 13\) and \(k = 1\text{.}\)
