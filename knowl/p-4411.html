Further analysis leads to a closed form expression for \(B(n)\text{,}\) which is
\begin{equation*} B(n) = \frac{1}{n+1}\left( \begin{array}{c} 2n \\ n \\ \end{array} \right) \end{equation*}
This sequence of numbers is often called the Catalan numbers. For more information on the Catalan numbers, see the entry A000108 in The On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences 1 .
