Item 8.3.4.3.c.
If \(f\) and \(g\) are permutations of \(A\text{,}\) then they are both injections and their composition, \(f\circ g\text{,}\) is a injection, by Theorem 8.3.6. By Theorem 8.3.7, \(f\circ g\) is also a surjection; therefore, \(f\circ g\) is a bijection on \(A\text{,}\) a permutation.
