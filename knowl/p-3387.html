Induction: Assume that for some \(n \geq 2\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} \left(a_1\bullet a_2\bullet \cdots \bullet a_n\right)^{-1}=a_n^{-1}\bullet \cdots \bullet a_2^{-1}\bullet a_1^{-1} \end{equation*}
We must show that
\begin{equation*} \left(a_1\bullet a_2\bullet \cdots \bullet a_n\bullet a_{n+1}\right)^{-1}=a_{n+1}^{-1}\bullet a_n^{-1}\bullet \cdots \bullet a_2^{-1}\bullet a_1^{-1} \end{equation*}
