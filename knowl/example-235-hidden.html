Let \(\Sigma = \ab\) , \(x=a\text{,}\) \(y=abaa\text{,}\) and \(z=bab\text{.}\) Then \(|x| = 1\text{,}\) \(|y| = 4\text{,}\) and \(|z|=3\text{.}\) Also, \(xx = aa\text{,}\) \(xy = aabaa\text{,}\) \(xz = abab\text{,}\) and \(zx = baba\text{.}\) Finally, \(x^R = a\text{,}\) \(y^R= aaba\text{,}\) and \(z^R=bab\text{.}\)
