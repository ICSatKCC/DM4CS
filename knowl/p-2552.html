Prove by induction that if \(n\geq 2\) and \(f_1, f_2 , \ldots , f_n\) are invertible functions on some nonempty set \(A\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation*} \left( f_1\circ f_2\circ \cdots \circ f_n \right){}^{-1}= f_n^{-1}\circ \cdots \circ f_2^{-1}\circ f_1^{-1}\text{.} \end{equation*}
