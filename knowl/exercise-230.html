Exercise 1.

State the dual of of each of the following:
  1. \(A \cup (B \cap A) = A\text{.}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle A \cup \overline{\left(\left(\overline{B} \cup A\right) \cap B\right)} = U\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \overline{\left(A \cup \overline{B}\right)} \cap B =\overline{A}\cap B\)
Answer.
  1. \(\displaystyle A\cap (B\cup A)=A\)
  2. \(\displaystyle A \cap \overline{\left(\left(\overline{B}\cap A\right)\cup B\right)}=\emptyset\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \overline{\left(A\cap \overline{B}\right)} \cup B=\overline{A}\cup B\)
